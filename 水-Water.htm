• 隶 (隸) lì
    Definition : Scribe

    The image is to grip and to write, and the word later gets the meaning of calligraphy.

  • 泉 quán
    Definition : Spring

    The water from a cave represents spring.

  • 沓 dá
    Definition : Compile/Repeat

    The running water over the mouth represents the repetition of words constantly.

  • 黎 lí
    Definition : Smooth

    The crop shows stability and smoothness of life.

  • 求 qiú
    Definition : Begging

    The fur coat means to take animals clothes, hence to beg.
