• 有 yǒu
    Definition : To have

    The character has two parts - hand and moon, with the hand taking the moon.

  • 肌 jī
    Definition : Muscle

    The Moon in the character means flesh and the counting word together mean a piece flesh, hence muscle.

  • 育 yù
    Definition : raise

    The original character is a woman raising a child.

  • 胃 wèi
    Definition : Stomach

    Stomach is in the center of the body in Chinese history which is the field feed the your flesh represented by the moon, hence the meaning Stomach.

  • 朋 péng
    Definition : Friend/Known

    Two like moons together, and moon is flesh, hence two people together.
