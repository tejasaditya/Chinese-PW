• 旦 dàn
    Definition : Daybreak/Dawn

    The line below the character for day represents the sun rising from the horizon hence giving it the meaning of dawn/daybreak.

  • 旧 (舊) jiù
    Definition : Worn out

    The original meaning of the character was fierce which slowly lost its meaning. Now the line before the radical 日(Rì)means that one day has passed, hence its definition being worn out.

  • 早 zǎo
    Definition : Morning

    The sun above the armor is a message to soldiers to wake up early.

  • 昌 chāng
    Definition : Good,flourishing,prosperous

    Two suns in the character represent a flourishing life.

  • 旱 hàn
    Definition : Dry

    The Sun dries the twig, hence dry is the meaning.
