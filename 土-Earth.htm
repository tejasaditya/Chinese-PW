• 在 zài
    Definition : To be at

    The image is of standing on the earth and hence existing there.

  • 地 de
    Definition : Ground

    The image is of the earth and a snake, which symbolizes animals growing from the ground.

  • 至 zhì
    Definition : To come back

    The arrow is coming back to the Earth and hence it means to come back.

  • 去 qù
    Definition : To Go

    The image is a caveman walking away from a cave, his home.

  • 圭 guī
    Definition : An elongated pointed tablet of jade held in the hands by ancient rulers on ceremonial occasions/Known

    Two rocks over each other symbolize the forming of jade.
